Research Document: Introduction to LangGraph

LangGraph is a framework for building stateful, multi-actor applications with Large Language Models (LLMs).

Key Features:
- State management for complex workflows
- Support for cyclic graphs
- Built on top of LangChain
- Excellent for multi-agent systems

Use Cases:
- Research agents that gather information iteratively
- Chatbots with memory and context
- Complex decision-making systems
